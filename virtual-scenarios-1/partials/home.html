Scenario Introduction

There are many computer-based information systems (CBIS) in hospitals, each of which has different purposes. This exercise will require you to identify the QSEN prelicensure and graduate-level knowledge, skills, and attitudes (KSAs) associated with each type of system. In addition, you will relate activities to the Informatics and Healthcare Technologies of AACN’s Essentials of Master’s Education in Nursing.
