Scenario Introduction

Look at Figure 14-1 on p. 237 of McGonigle and Mastrian. Part of the workflow process for the ED shows a decision point as to whether a bed is available. Assume that your hospital has been experiencing a large buildup of patients in its emergency department (ED) because there are no beds indicated as available. However, you note that there are several empty rooms on your floor. You decide to run a report showing the length of time that occurs between when a patient is discharged and when housekeeping enters a code that signals to admitting that the room has been cleaned and is available for occupancy.
